Material: Felt

Doing "Good influence begins from my hands" campaign, I compared it to stretchmarks to visualize the expansion of influence. Stretchmarks expand skin, but there is no pain and hard to recognize. Good influence grows our sense of rights, but there is no real or potential damage. This is a good influence that I redefined. A gap was needed to produce new skin. A soft wool embrace the gap warmly and the work is finally completed.
